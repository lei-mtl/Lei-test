Behind the Lens

Hello, I'm Alex Lumina.

Photography is more than just clicking a shutter; it's about freezing a moment in time that tells a story without uttering a single word. Based in the Pacific Northwest, I specialize in capturing the raw beauty of nature and the intricate emotions of the human experience.

With over a decade of experience, my work has been featured in major publications and galleries globally. I believe in natural light, genuine expressions, and the power of visual narrative.

Whether I'm hiking up a mountain at dawn or shooting a quiet portrait session in the studio, my goal remains the same: to create art that resonates.
